Clash Royale Deck Matchup Visualizations

This project visualizes **Clash Royale** deck matchups using **PCA** and **t-SNE** projections. It includes two main types of visualizations:

Individual Matchup Distributions

These charts show the distribution of individual deck matchups, highlighting how different cards and strategies perform against each other.

  • Winner's First Alphabetical Win Condition
  • Loser's First Alphabetical Win Condition
  • Average Elixir Cost Difference
  • Tuple of Winner and Loser First Alphabetical Win Condition
  • Winner's Shared Cards, K = 100
  • Loser's Shared Cards, K = 100
  • Winner's Shared Cards, K = 500
  • Loser's Shared Cards, K = 500
  • Shared Cards, K = 1000
  • Loser's Shared Cards, K = 1000

Deck Distributions

These charts show the distribution of entire decks, grouped by various features like win conditions, elixir cost, and shared card similarity.

  • Deck Distribution By Winner
  • Deck Distribution By Tower Card
  • Deck Distribution By Elixir Cost
  • Deck Distribution By Win Condition
  • Deck Distribution By 1 Shared Card, KNN=10
  • Deck Distribution By 2 Shared Cards, KNN=10

Each chart provides a unique perspective on the relationships between cards, their average elixir cost, and the strategies that lead to victory. Click on a link to explore these insights.
